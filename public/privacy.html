Privacy Policy

Your health data is your own. Here is how we handle it.

1. No Permanent Storage

We do not store your blood reports on our servers. Once the analysis is complete, the uploaded file is immediately deleted.

2. Encrypted Processing

All data sent between your browser and our server is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols to ensure your information remains private.

3. AI Processing

Reports are processed using the Gemini API. No personally identifiable information is used for training the AI model.

4. Local Execution

The application runs locally on your machine, ensuring that your data stays within your controlled environment.
